About Gokkens

Gokkens Construction Company Nig. Ltd is a fully registered Nigerian firm, incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020 with RC No. 7350581.

Headquartered at No. 1, BSC Road, Orlu, Imo State with an additional office at #205 Jubocs Mall, New Owerri.

Our Leadership

A Vision Built to Last

Prince Kenneth Obi is the founding Managing Director and CEO of Gokkens Construction Company Nig. Ltd. A visionary builder and entrepreneur from Imo State, Prince Kenneth has dedicated his professional life to transforming the built environment of southeastern Nigeria.

With a deep understanding of infrastructure development and an unwavering belief in Nigerian potential, he established Gokkens in 2024 with a clear mandate — to deliver construction excellence that communities can trust. His leadership philosophy is rooted in integrity, accountability, and an obsessive commitment to quality.

Under his direction, Gokkens has taken on ambitious projects in road construction, drainage engineering, and real estate, earning a reputation for delivering on promises.

"We don't just build structures — we build legacies. Every contract we take on is a commitment to the people who will use what we create for generations to come."
